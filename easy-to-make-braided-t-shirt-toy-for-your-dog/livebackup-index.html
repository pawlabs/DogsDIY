Easy to Make Braided T-shirt Toy for Your Dog

Dogs are known for how much they love to play. This simply points to the fact that they love to have playthings. DIY toys go a long way in strengthening your bond with your pet. A braided t-shirt toy with your scent still on it is just about one of the best gifts you can give your pet. And you really don't want your couch or your favorite shirt that you left lying around to become your dog's toy.

There are certain dog toys that are super easy to make at home especially if you’re a DIYer. A braided t-shirt toy is a perfect example of this and makes an even more perfect toy for your pet. It is easy to make, requires the most basic materials, and more importantly, is safe for your dog to play with.

DIY Braided Dog T-shirt Toy

  • DIFFICULTY: Easy
  • CRAFTING TIME: 15 to 45 minutes
  • USED FOR: Dogs are very active and love to play a whole lot. Providing them with a toy makes your pet very happy and excited just as you definitely want them to be. These toys cost nothing, so you save money and more important, they are safe. Some factory-made dog toys comprise of some harmful manufacture material, so, a DIY toy like this is totally safe for your dog.

WHAT YOU NEED:

A pair of scissors

2-3 old T-shirts (preferably cotton)

DIRECTIONS:

Step 1:

With your pair of scissors, cut off the hem of the T-shirts.

Step 2:

Next, cut little 2-3” wide slits evenly along the base of the shirts. After cutting, rip them along the slits that you made so that you have various strips.

Step 3:

Gather the strips together and tie off one end, then divide the strips into thirds.

Step 4:

Braid from the end that you tied, down to the untied end, and ensure that your braids are tight.

Step 5:

After braiding to the bottom, tie up the other end and cut off any unnecessary strips of fabric still present.

There you have it, the perfect toy for your pet!

Reminder:

This toy is indestructible, so, if it is worn from chewing after a while, you can throw it away at your own discretion and make a brand new one!
